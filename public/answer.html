Here's a simple method for making delicious chicken:

Basic Roasted Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken (3-4 lbs) or chicken pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or melted butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional seasonings: garlic powder, paprika, herbs (thyme, rosemary)

Instructions:

  1. Prep (20 min before cooking):

    • Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
    • Pat chicken dry with paper towels
    • Rub with oil/butter and season generously

  2. Cook:

    • Place in roasting pan
    • Roast 20 minutes per pound (about 60-80 min for whole chicken)
    • Internal temp should reach 165°F (74°C)

  3. Rest:

    • Let sit 5-10 minutes before carving

Quick Pan-Seared Chicken Breast:

  • Season chicken breasts
  • Heat oil in pan over medium-high heat
  • Cook 6-7 minutes per side until golden
  • Check internal temp reaches 165°F

Tips:

  • Don't overcook (it becomes dry)
  • Use a meat thermometer for accuracy
  • Let meat rest after cooking
